Bo Liu received the B.Eng. degree and Ph. D. degrees from the Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China, in 2017 and 2025, respectively. He is currently an assistant researcher at the Department of Control Science and Engineering at Harbin Institute of Technology. His research interest covers distributed planning and safety control, cooperative navigation, as well as cooperative positioning for intelligent unmanned systems. He has published 15 SCI papers in high-level academic journals such as IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering, IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Electronics, and Nonlinear Dynamics. He has also been granted 8 Chinese invention patents, and won more than a dozen national awards in various science and technology competitions. A more detailed personal data can be found in [CV](https://hitlb17.github.io/cv/).
